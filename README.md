# Customer-Churn-Analysis

Customer Churn Analysis
Project Title / Headline

Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Oracle SQL & Power BI | Customer Retention & Business Insights

Short Description / Purpose

Developed an interactive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard using Oracle SQL and Power BI to analyze customer retention, identify churn patterns, and uncover the key factors influencing customer attrition.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor customer behavior, evaluate churn trends across different customer segments, analyze contract and payment preferences, and make data-driven decisions to improve customer retention strategies.

Business Impact & Insights
Enabled business stakeholders to monitor customer retention through an interactive dashboard.
Identified Month-to-Month contracts as the highest churn segment.
Discovered that Fiber Optic customers experience higher churn compared to DSL users.
Highlighted Electronic Check as the most common payment method among churned customers.
Revealed that customers with shorter tenure are more likely to discontinue the service.
Provided actionable recommendations to improve customer retention and reduce churn.
Reduced manual reporting by consolidating customer data into a single interactive Power BI dashboard.
Improved business decision-making through dynamic filters, KPIs, and business insights.

Dashboard Overview

The Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of customer retention and churn performance through interactive KPI cards, business charts, and dynamic filters.

The dashboard displays essential business metrics including:

👥 Total Customers (7,043)
🟢 Active Customers (5.2K)
🔴 Churned Customers (1.9K)
📉 Churn Rate (26.54%)
💰 Average Monthly Charges (64.76)
📅 Average Customer Tenure (32.37 Months)

Users can analyze customer churn by:

Contract Type
Payment Method
Internet Service
Gender
Customer Tenure

Interactive slicers allow users to filter the dashboard based on customer attributes, while a dedicated Business Insights page summarizes key findings and provides actionable recommendations to support customer retention strategies.

The dashboard helps organizations identify high-risk customer segments, optimize retention initiatives, and make informed business decisions using data-driven insights.
